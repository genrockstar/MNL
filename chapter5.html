THE COLISEUM BATTLES

Listen:

The moon cast long shadows across the ancient ruins. On one side stood Ernel, the mystic mage. Opposite him was Karle, adjusting his glasses. "Your gadgets are no match for the ancient arts," Ernel declared. Karle smirked. "We’ll see about that." The decisive moment came when Karle hurled his Smartphone Strike, blasting Ernel into defeat.

Jai vs. Missy

Missy blurred forward with Deadline Rush, shouting, "Time is money, Jai!" But Jai remained calm. When Missy hurled her Document Blast, Jai’s Lotus Orb reflected it back. THWACK! Missy was struck by her own attack. Jai followed with Guardian Angel, healing himself and knocking her down.

Rus vs. Denice

Denice danced across the arena, using her Heritage Fan to silence Rus. But Rus shook it off. His Jade Carapace reflected her damage. "Nowhere left to run!" Rus roared, unleashing Emerald Overgrowth to root her completely. Denice raised her hand: "I can’t move! I give up!"

The Revelation

Karle sat alone in the preparation chamber. Behind him, a voice broke the silence. "Hey." The Wolf stood there, removing his mask. The face beneath was one Karle knew. "Pablo…" A storm of emotions surged through him—joy, shock, anger. His father was the Wolf.

Semi-Finals: Karle vs. Karlos

Karle faced Karlos, the Gymnastic Dynamo. Karlos launched a Tumble Assault, but Karle didn't panic. He triggered Urban Dash. When Karlos used Gravity Defy to become a golden comet, Karle waited. Fueled by the truth of Pablo’s identity, Karle fought with clarity. Brain beat brawn. Winner: Karle.

"The chapter closed with an unspoken bond between father and son."
